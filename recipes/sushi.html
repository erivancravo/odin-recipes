Sushi

The sushi recipe:

Description:

This homemade sushi is so simple and
fun to make! It is definitely sushi
for those who may be nervous to try
sushi! This also makes a great appetizer
and my whole family loves them! Most
of these ingredients can be found in
your local supermarketand the result
is yum-yum-yum! Serve with a bowl of
soy sauceand a lump of wasabi.

Ingredients:

  • 2/3 cup of water
  • 1/3 cup medium-grain white rice, uncooked
  • 6 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 4 sheets nori (dry seaweed)
  • 4 ounces smoked salmon, cut into strips
  • 2 ounces cold cream cheese, cut into thin strips
  • 4 scallions, sliced into thin strips

Steps

  1. Bring water and rice to a boil in a saucepan.
    Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer
    until rice is tender and water has been absorbed,
    20 to 25 minutes. Remove from the heat and cool,
    about 10 minutes. Toss with vinegar.

  2. Place 1 sheet of nori on a rolling mat, layer
    with 1/4 of the rice, and press rice out to the
    edges. Lay 1/4 of the salmon, 1/4 of the cream
    cheese, and 1/4 of the scallions in the center. Roll
    up carefully and wrap in plastic wrap. Repeat with
    remaining nori, rice, salmon, cream cheese, and
    scallions. Place in the refrigerator until chilled,
    at least 1 hour.

  3. Remove sushi rolls from the refrigerator,
    discard plastic wrap, and cut each roll into
    6 slices. Layer all rolls on a platter.
